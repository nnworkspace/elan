# Glossary

| Term                               | Explanation                                                                                                                                                                                |
| ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Digital Euro**                   | A digital form of central bank money issued by the Eurosystem, intended to complement cash and provide a secure, widely accessible means of payment in the digital age.                    |
| **Eurosystem**                     | The monetary authority of the euro area, comprising the European Central Bank (ECB) and the national central banks (NCBs) of the euro area countries.                                      |
| **European Central Bank (ECB)**    | The central institution responsible for monetary policy in the euro area and for coordinating the design and issuance of the digital euro.                                                 |
| **National Central Bank (NCB)**    | A central bank of a euro area Member State that participates in the Eurosystem and may be responsible for specific operational or technical components of the digital euro.                |
| **Payment Service Provider (PSP)** | A regulated entity (such as a bank or payment institution) that provides payment services to end users and is expected to distribute the digital euro to consumers and merchants.          |
| **Scheme Rulebook**                | A formal set of rules defining roles, obligations, processes, and interoperability requirements for participants in the digital euro ecosystem.                                            |
| **Offline Payments**               | Digital euro payments that can be executed without an active internet connection, typically involving secure hardware and cryptographic safeguards to prevent double spending.             |
| **Issuance Component**             | The technical and operational mechanisms through which digital euro units are created, managed, and redeemed by the Eurosystem.                                                            |
| **Distribution Component**         | The part of the digital euro system responsible for making digital euro available to end users, primarily via PSPs, while respecting Eurosystem control and rules.                         |
| **Monetary Sovereignty**           | The ability of a currency issuer to maintain control over its monetary system, including payment infrastructure, data, and policy transmission, without undue reliance on external actors. |
